SPECIAL SURGICAL CAMP FOR GOITER ORGANIZED

Monday, 21 January, 2019

In partnership with US based NGO named Medical Mission for Children and Helping Hands for Rwanda,

RMH is conducting a special surgical camp to help patients with goiter issues.

The camp is being facilitated with RMH ENT doctors together with 13 health practitioners from US.

RMH SPREADS FESTIVE JOY AMONG HOSPITALIZED CHILDREN

Monday, 22 January, 2019

As has always done for the past years RMH has once again organized a Christmas party for hospitalized children. The party organized at the Pediatrics department.

Attended by around 100 people many of whom were children who came to give cheerful joy to others who were hospitalized.
